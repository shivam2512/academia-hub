## 1. Getting Started — Login

### How to Access the Platform
1. Open your browser (Google Chrome recommended) and go to:
   **https://lms.digitalbusinesssolutionsforyou.com**
2. Click the **"Sign In"** or **"Student Portal"** button on the homepage.
3. Enter your registered **email address** and **password**.
4. Click **Login**. You will be automatically taken to your dashboard based on your role.

> [!IMPORTANT]
> Your account is **created and managed by the Admin**. You **cannot self-register**. Contact your administrator if you don't have login credentials.

> [!WARNING]
> There is **no self-service "Forgot Password" option**. If you forget your password or are locked out of your account, you must **contact the Admin** to have it reset for you.

## 2. Student Guide

### 2.1 Dashboard Overview
After logging in, your **Student Dashboard** shows:
- 🎯 **Next Live Class** — A highlighted card at the top with a direct **"Join Class"** button
- 📊 **Stats** — Number of enrolled batches, upcoming classes, new notes, and new recordings
- 👤 **Your Profile Card** — Your personal details, enrollment info, and batch assignment
- 📚 **My Batches** — Quick links to all batches you are enrolled in
- 📄 **Latest Notes** — Recently shared study materials
- 🎬 **Latest Recordings** — Recently uploaded class recordings

---

### 2.2 Joining a Live Class
1. On your dashboard, look for the **"Next Live Class"** banner at the top.
2. Click the **"Join Class"** button — this will open your Zoom/Google Meet link directly in a new tab.
3. If you don't see the banner, there are no upcoming classes scheduled yet. Check back later.

> [!TIP]
> You can also go to **Batches → Your Batch → Live Classes** to see all upcoming and past classes.

---

### 2.3 Accessing Notes & Study Materials
1. From the left sidebar or dashboard, click on **"Batches"**.
2. Click on your enrolled batch (e.g., "IT Batch — October 2026").
3. Click the **"Notes"** tab inside the batch.
4. You will see all uploaded notes with their title and description.
5. Click on a note to **view or download** it.

---

### 2.4 Watching Recorded Videos
1. Go to **Batches** → Click your batch → Click the **"Videos"** tab.
2. You will see a list of all recorded sessions.
3. Click on a video to watch it directly in the browser.

> [!NOTE]
> Video content is protected. You cannot download or share video links outside the platform.

---

### 2.5 Batch Chat
1. Go to **Batches** → Click your batch → Click the **"Chat"** tab.
2. Type your message in the text box at the bottom and press **Enter** or click **Send**.
3. You can communicate in real-time with your batchmates and teachers here.

> [!IMPORTANT]
> Keep all chat communication **respectful and academic**. Sharing of personal contact details or external links is not recommended.

---

### 2.6 Viewing & Editing Your Profile
1. Click on **"Profile"** from the sidebar or from your dashboard card.
2. You can update:
   - Full Name
   - Mobile & WhatsApp Number
   - City and State
   - Education Details
   - Profile Photo
3. Click **"Save"** after making changes.

## 3. Teacher Guide

### 3.1 Dashboard Overview
Your **Teacher Dashboard** shows:
- 📅 **Your Upcoming Classes** — All classes scheduled across your assigned batches
- 📖 **Recently Taught** — A history of past classes
- 📊 **Stats** — Number of batches, students, notes uploaded, and recordings added

---

### 3.2 Scheduling a Live Class
1. Go to **Batches** from the sidebar.
2. Click on the batch you want to schedule a class for.
3. Click the **"Live Classes"** tab.
4. Click **"Schedule Class"** (the `+` button).
5. Fill in the details:
   - **Title** — e.g., "Java OOPs Concepts — Session 3"
   - **Scheduled Date & Time**
   - **Duration** (in minutes)
   - **Meeting URL** — Paste your Zoom or Google Meet link here
6. Click **"Save"**. The class will now appear on all enrolled students' dashboards.

---

### 3.3 Uploading Notes
1. Go to **Batches** → Click your batch → Click the **"Notes"** tab.
2. Click the **"Add Note"** (`+`) button.
3. Fill in:
   - **Title** — e.g., "Module 3 — Java Collections"
   - **Description** (optional)
   - **File** — Upload a PDF, Word document, or image
4. Click **"Upload"**. Students in this batch can now access the note.

---

### 3.4 Uploading Video Recordings
1. Go to **Batches** → Click your batch → Click the **"Videos"** tab.
2. Click **"Add Recording"** (`+`).
3. Fill in:
   - **Title** — e.g., "Day 5 — SQL Joins Explained"
   - **Video URL** — Paste the YouTube (unlisted), Google Drive, or direct video link
   - **Description** (optional)
4. Click **"Save"**.

---

### 3.5 Participating in Batch Chat
- Go to **Batches** → Your Batch → **"Chat"** tab.
- You can respond to student questions and share quick announcements here.

## 4. Admin Guide

### 4.1 Dashboard Overview
Your **Admin Console** shows a live summary of:
| Metric | Description |
|---|---|
| Batches | Total number of active batches |
| Total Users | All registered users on the platform |
| Pending Fees | Total outstanding payment amount across all students |
| Students | Total number of students |
| Live Classes | Total classes scheduled |
| Notes | Total notes uploaded |
| Recordings | Total recordings added |

Below the stats, you'll see **Upcoming Live Classes** across all batches.

---

### 4.2 Managing Users
1. Click **"Users"** from the sidebar.
2. You can:
   - **View** all registered users with their role, email, and joining date
   - **Add a new user** by clicking **"Add User"** — Enter their email and assign a role (Student / Teacher / Admin)
   - **Edit** a user's details or role
   - **Delete** a user (⚠️ this is permanent)

> [!WARNING]
> Deleting a user removes their access immediately but does NOT delete their data (notes, chat history, etc.).

---

### 4.3 Managing Batches
1. Click **"Batches"** from the sidebar.
2. Click **"New Batch"** to create a batch.
3. Fill in:
   - **Name** — e.g., "Full Stack Java — Oct 2026 Morning"
   - **Batch Month** — e.g., "October 2026"
   - **Subject** — e.g., "Java, SQL, Python"
   - **Description** (optional)
4. Click **"Create"**.

To **edit or delete** a batch, click the **⋮ (three dots)** menu on any batch card and select **Edit** or **Delete**.

> [!CAUTION]
> Deleting a batch permanently removes ALL data associated with it — including notes, recordings, live class schedules, and chat history. This action cannot be undone.

---

### 4.4 Adding Members to a Batch
1. Open any batch and go to the **batch detail** page.
2. Find the **"Members"** section.
3. Click **"Add Member"** and search for the user by name or email.
4. Assign their role within the batch: **Student** or **Teacher**.
5. Click **"Add"**.

---

### 4.5 Managing Invoices & Fees
1. Click **"Invoices"** from the sidebar.
2. You can:
   - View all student invoices with total fee, amount paid, and pending balance
   - **Create a new invoice** for a student — Enter total fee, course details, and payment installments
   - **Record a payment** against an existing invoice
   - **Download invoice** as a PDF for sharing with students

---

### 4.6 Managing Roles
1. Click **"Roles"** from the sidebar.
2. Here you can assign or revoke system-level roles (**Admin**, **Teacher**, **Student**) for any user.

> [!CAUTION]
> Assigning the **Admin** role gives that user full control over the entire platform including the ability to delete data and other users. Only assign this to trusted individuals.

## 6. Do's and Don'ts

| ✅ Do | ❌ Don't |
|---|---|
| Use a strong, unique password | Share your login credentials with anyone |
| Sign out after every session on shared devices | Leave your account logged in on public computers |
| Keep chat communication academic and respectful | Share offensive content, personal contact details, or external links in chat |
| Use the latest version of Google Chrome for best experience | Use outdated browsers like Internet Explorer |
| Contact admin if you face any access issues | Attempt to access sections outside your assigned role |
| Report bugs or issues to the admin via email | Try to inspect or reverse-engineer the application |

## 7. Troubleshooting

| Problem | Solution |
|---|---|
| **"Something went wrong" error on loading** | Refresh the page. If the issue persists, clear your browser cache (Ctrl+Shift+Delete) and try again. |
| **Can't see any batches** | Your account has not been added to a batch yet. Contact your admin. |
| **Live class button missing** | No class has been scheduled yet. Check back or ask your teacher. |
| **Video not playing** | Check your internet connection. Try a different browser. |
| **Can't login / forgot password** | Contact the Admin directly. There is no self-service password reset. The Admin will reset your password and share the new credentials with you. |
| **Profile changes not saving** | Ensure all required fields are filled in and try again. |
| **Chat messages not loading** | Refresh the page. If the issue persists, clear browser cache. |
| **Page showing "Not Secure"** | This is a temporary browser cache issue. Try opening in an Incognito window or wait a few minutes. |
